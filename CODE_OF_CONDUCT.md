# Code of Conduct

Gravewright is built around technical collaboration. Participants are expected to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus criticism on code, design, tests, and documentation.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about risks and tradeoffs.
- Give reproducible bug reports and actionable review feedback.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Abusive behavior in issues, reviews, discussions, or community spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to the maintainers privately. Include relevant links, screenshots, dates, and context when possible. Do not publish private information in public issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, lock, or remove contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent loss of project participation privileges.
